Privacy Policy — PracticeHub → PIMS Copier

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Overview

PracticeHub → PIMS Copier is a Chrome extension that copies prescription data from the Chewy PracticeHub website to your clipboard. Your privacy is important to us, and this extension is designed to operate entirely within your browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. No data is sent to any external server, analytics service, or third party.

Website Content Access

The extension reads text content from the PracticeHub prescription review page (chewyhealth.com) solely to extract prescription field data when you click the "Copy to PIMS" button. This data is copied directly to your local clipboard and is never transmitted externally.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's built-in sync storage (chrome.storage.sync) to save your preferences, such as which fields to copy, separator format, and display settings. These preferences are synced across your Chrome profile by Google's infrastructure and are not accessible to the extension developer.

Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function: access to chewyhealth.com pages to read prescription data, clipboard write access to copy data, and storage access to save your preferences.

Third Parties

This extension does not use any third-party libraries, analytics tools, tracking scripts, or external services. All code runs locally in your browser.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
