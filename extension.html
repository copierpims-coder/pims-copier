Setup
Install in under a minute
1

Add to Chrome

Click "Add to Chrome" above, or download the ZIP from GitHub and load it unpacked.

2

Open any prescription in PracticeHub

Click Review on any prescription — the Copy to PIMS button appears next to View PDF automatically.

3

Click and paste

Click Copy to PIMS, then paste into your PIMS with Ctrl+V. Done.

4

Customize your fields

Click the extension icon in your toolbar to choose which fields to include and your preferred format.

⚠️ Always verify. This tool copies data from the PracticeHub screen as a workflow convenience. Always confirm copied fields against the original PracticeHub record before saving to your PIMS.
